- XP gain
For each Fakeymon enemy E defeated, the winning Fakeymon W earns sum(stats(E))/10 XP points.

- Obtaining new monsters
For each Fakeymon defeated, there is a 50% chance that the mon will want to join the team.

- Item drops
For each Fakeymon defeated, there is a 75% chance that the mon will drop an object.
    Of this 75%, there is a 50% chance that the object is a cash amount from $1 to $15 at random.
    The other 50% chance is dropping an item.
Additionally, there is a 50% chance that the Fakeymon is holding an item. If it joins a player's team,
    the player can take this item. Therefore the overall chance of obtaining an item after battle is ~53.13%.
